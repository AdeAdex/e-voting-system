Voting Instructions & Help

Overview

This electronic voting (e-voting) system is developed to ease the stress of having to stand at the polling unit and also waiting for some days before the winner is decided.

This system focus on many steps or procedures that the voters has to take before a successful voting ✅ but before that, let look into this very important note.

There are many different technologies that can be used to support the electoral process. This guide will focus on electronic technologies that assist voting and the subsequent counting of votes.

When discussing electronic voting, two separate, but sometimes related technologies are generally referred to – electronic voting and electronic counting. The traditional paper-based voting system consists of a voter manually marking the paper ballot and then the ballot being counted by hand by election officials. In elections using electronic voting or counting technologies, one or both of these processes are automated electronically.

Registration Guides

Kindly fill out the registration form and the system will look up your voter registration status. Remember you can't vote unless you've registered and you can't register if you are below the age of 18. Our system check properly using your National Identification to confirm your age and once you are eligible, you can then proceed with the registration. Keep in mind that any state of origin you select during the registration, thats the state you will be able to vote when it come to governorship election.

Take a few minutes to verify your registration status today. Also note that after a successful registration, voters will be given a unique ID and a special key that he/she can use to login to the voting portal.

Note: Save your ID and the Key that the system will generate for you as failure to remember them maybe disqualified you from voting.

Voting Methods

With a registered and given ID and Key, login and you will be directed to the voting portal and in the voting portal, you will be provided with the types of election depending on the election. For example,
  • Presidential
  • Governorship
  • House of Rep
  • State Honorable
  • etc.
Click on the election type you wish to vote first and in the next window, where election contestant names, party logo and name appears, there's a voting button at the right side of every contestant, click on any of your choice and your vote is recorded automatically, click my choice button to view your vote choice and if you wish to change it again click the fingerprint button of your choice again and click on print to have a copy of the vote, click next button to vote in the next election type and once you are done, click on the finish button to end the vote for the next voter. Note: Once you've vote, you can't come back to vote again. Any such steps will be treated as illegal.
